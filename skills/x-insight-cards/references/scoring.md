# Scoring rubric

Score each verified candidate out of 100:

| Dimension | Maximum | Question |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Insight gain | 30 | Does it add a reusable mental model or non-obvious distinction? |
| Clarity | 20 | Can the central idea be understood quickly? |
| Chinese social fit | 20 | Will the idea remain useful and natural after translation? |
| Source credibility | 15 | Is the author/source identifiable and trustworthy for this claim? |
| Freshness | 10 | Is it recent, timely, or still meaningfully evergreen? |
| Visual readability | 5 | Can it fit a readable card without destructive editing? |

## Selection rules

- Verify before scoring.
- Reject candidates below 75.
- Sort by total score, then insight gain, credibility, and freshness.
- Treat views as supporting evidence, never as a substitute for quality.
- Avoid selecting five versions of the same idea in one run.
- Record component scores so another reviewer can audit the ranking.
